    I’m Ronit Murpani, a software engineer with about 1.5 years of experience, mainly working with Python, Generative AI, and backend systems.

    What people usually associate me with is ownership. If something is unclear or broken, I don’t wait for perfect instructions, I try to understand the problem end to end and move it forward with a solution-first mindset. I also tend to pick up new tools and domains pretty fast, especially when the problem is real-world and high impact.

    In my last role, I worked as an AI Software Engineer at LUME Global, where we were building a skill-tech platform for the Government of Oman. It was a fairly high-pressure project because we built and shipped the entire platform in around 4 to 5 months, and it’s now actively used by over 10,000users.

    My core contribution there was on the Generative AI side. I worked on designing and implementing AI-driven features that addressed actual user pain points, not just demo use cases. That involved prompt engineering, backend integration, and making sure the AI features were reliable and scalable in a production environment.

    Overall, I enjoy working on problems where I can combine engineering fundamentals with AI to build things that are actually used. At this stage of my career, I’m looking for roles where I can take more ownership, deepen my backend and AI expertise, and contribute to products that have visible impact.
